Description of Business Idea

I am looking staring a small scale business information technology related and my option is open a Cyber Cafe. An Internet café, which is also known as a cybercafé, is a place which provides Internet access to the public, usually for a fee. Most internet cafés usually provide snacks and drinks, hence the café in the name.

Product & Service

In an internet café, the fee for using a computer that is connected to an internet facility is usually charged as a time-based rate. Now a days printing and scan any documents cyber café charged fee and also internet calling and gaming zone is a source of income in a cyber café. Many cyber café have also small restaurant inside cyber café so they can earn selling some snack, café and fast food item. So I will start a cyber café with a small restaurant and my business will offering like below this:
  • Providing fast and secured internet browsing services
  • Providing cheap and secured international internet calls
  • Providing fast and secured teleconference services
  • Providing document scanning and photocopy services
  • Sale of soft drinks, coffee and snacks
  • Sale of call cards, and internet bundles from various telecom service providers
  • Providing related internet café advisory and consultancy services
